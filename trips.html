Family Fun Float

Perfect for beginners and families, this scenic 5-mile stretch offers calm waters and beautiful canyon views. Enjoy wildlife sightings and plenty of splash zones without the intense rapids!

  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Price: $45 per person

Adventurer’s Rush

Our most popular trip! Tackle Class II–III rapids through stunning mountain scenery. Great for those looking for excitement with a balance of challenge and fun.

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Price: $85 per person

Extreme Rapids Expedition

For the fearless! Take on intense Class IV–V rapids with expert guides. Expect drops, waves, and unforgettable adrenaline. Experience required.

  • Duration: Full day
  • Difficulty: Advanced
  • Price: $150 per person
